School Notifications

The health of our campus community, and the public, is of paramount importance to Howard University. In light of the global Coronavirus Disease-2019 (COVID-19) concerns and the recent impacts of the public health situation on the Washington, D.C. area, the Office of Registrar will be disabling pickup and mail transcript delivery options. We do sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused. We will continue to monitor the situation as it progresses and provide updates via our Registrar webpage.

COST AND PAYMENT METHOD

  • All Howard University Transcripts are $8.25 per copy.
  • You can pay for your transcript order with any major credit or debit card. Your card is not charged until we send your transcript(s). However, if you use a debit card, your bank may put a hold on your funds when we pre-authorize your payment. If you have questions on the pre-authorization, please contact your bank.

TRANSCRIPT ORDERING TRACKING

Order updates will be sent to you via email and if you choose via text messages. You can also track your transcript order online.

PROCESSING TIME AND DELIVERY

  • Transcript requests are processed in the order they are received. Normal processing time is 2 business days. However, requests received for individuals who attended prior to 1985 may take 3 to 5 business days.
